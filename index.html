Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about affordable screen repair, pricing, and service across Central Florida.

What is the cheapest way to repair a pool screen in Central Florida?+
The most affordable option is usually patching or re-screening only the damaged panels instead of the entire enclosure. We inspect your lanai for free, tell you honestly which panels actually need replacement, and give you an upfront flat price before we start — no surprise fees.
Do you offer affordable or cheap screen repair in Davenport and Kissimmee?+
Yes. We offer special community pricing for Davenport neighborhoods like Solterra, ChampionsGate, and Providence, plus competitive rates throughout Kissimmee and all of Central Florida. Message us on WhatsApp to ask about current pricing.
Can you repair storm or hail damaged screens the same day?+
Often, yes. If you reach out early in the day and we have the right screen material in stock, we can typically complete same-day repairs, especially for single panels or screen doors. Larger jobs may be scheduled within 24-48 hours.
How much does it cost to re-screen a full pool enclosure?+
Cost depends on the size of your cage, screen height, and the mesh type you choose (standard, pet-resistant, or super screen). Send us a few photos on WhatsApp and we'll give you a free, no-obligation estimate.
What areas of Central Florida do you serve?+
We serve all of Central Florida, with a strong focus on Davenport, Kissimmee, Champions Gate, Haines City, Winter Haven, Clermont, and Orlando. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just ask.
Do you also repair screen doors and bent frames?+
Yes, in addition to mesh panel replacement we repair bent or corroded aluminum framing, screen door rollers and closers, and spline replacement for a tight, lasting fit.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Yes, Gabriel Lanai Screens is licensed and insured, giving you peace of mind for work done on your home.
How do I get a quote?+
The fastest way is WhatsApp: tap the green button, send a photo of the damage and your address, and we'll reply with an estimate — usually within minutes during business hours.
